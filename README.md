Byte Types -


Jump Bytes -
Triple Jump - Give an additional jump.
Hover Jump - Disables the ability to use Double and Triple jump in exchange for the ability to momentarily hover around mid air. (Can be paired with High Jump.)(Glide)
High Jump - Jumps come out slower, but have a significant boost in verticality. (Is compatible with Double and Triple jump, but only affects the first jump.[Cannot use the double/triple jump if Hover Jump is equipped])


Boost Bytes - 
Dodge Boost - Allow you to dash past/through enemies without taking damage. (Not compatible with Power Boost.)
Extended Boost - Allows you to continuously boost as long as the boost button is pressed.
Power Boost - Allows you to charge into and damage enemies and obstacles without taking damage. (Not compatible with Dodge Boost.)


Attack Bytes -
Blaster -  lower firing rate and range compared to default in exchange for larger/more powerful bullets, can punt you (shotgun)
Rapid Fire - upped bullet firing rate, higher collective damage, normal range, have to charge it (like a gatling gun)
Homing Shot - slightly weaker damage then normal, but has longer range that locks onto targets, normal fire rate. (Maybe it has a charge shot that is bigger/stronger?)

* City Streets - Returning to find clues, Learns the Ennead may have Solanna, fight the boss to get additional info.
